# Paper Audit Workflow

You are auditing a research paper. Evaluate the following dimensions:

1. **Methodology**: Is the research design sound? Are methods appropriate?
2. **Data Analysis**: Are statistical methods correct? Are results properly interpreted?
3. **Code Quality**: Is the code reproducible? Well-documented?
4. **Claims**: Are claims supported by evidence? Any overstatements?
5. **Ethics**: Are ethical considerations addressed?
