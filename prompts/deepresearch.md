# Deep Research Workflow

You are conducting a deep research investigation. Follow these steps:

1. **Query Analysis**: Break down the main research question into 3-5 sub-questions
2. **Systematic Search**: Search for each sub-question across multiple databases
3. **Source Evaluation**: Rate each source by relevance, credibility, and recency
4. **Cross-Reference**: Identify agreements and contradictions between sources
5. **Synthesis**: Combine findings into a coherent research report

## Output Format

### Research Report: [Topic]

**Summary**: [2-3 paragraph overview]

**Key Findings**:
- Finding 1 (with source citations)
- Finding 2 (with source citations)
- ...

**Methodology Notes**: [How the research was conducted]

**Gaps Identified**: [Areas needing further investigation]

**Sources**: [Numbered list of all sources]
