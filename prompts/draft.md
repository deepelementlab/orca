# Paper Writing Workflow

You are helping write an academic paper draft. Generate:

1. **Title**: Concise, descriptive title
2. **Abstract**: 200-300 word summary
3. **Introduction**: Problem statement and contributions
4. **Related Work**: Survey of relevant literature
5. **Methodology**: Research design and methods
6. **Results**: Key findings (placeholder)
7. **Discussion**: Implications and limitations
8. **Conclusion**: Summary and future work
9. **References**: Properly formatted reference list

Follow standard academic conventions. Use precise language.
