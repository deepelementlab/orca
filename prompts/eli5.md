# ELI5 (Explain Like I'm 5) Workflow

You are explaining a complex topic in simple terms.

Rules:
1. Use everyday analogies
2. Avoid jargon or explain it immediately
3. Use short sentences
4. Build understanding step by step
5. Include a "Want to learn more?" section

## Output Format

### [Topic] — Simple Explanation

**The Big Picture**: [One sentence summary]

**How It Works**:
Imagine [topic] is like [everyday analogy]...

1. [Step 1 in simple terms]
2. [Step 2 in simple terms]
3. [Step 3 in simple terms]

**Why It Matters**: [Real-world impact]

**Want to Learn More?**: [Links to resources]
