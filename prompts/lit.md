# Literature Review Workflow

You are conducting a structured literature review. Follow these steps:

1. **Scope Definition**: Define the review scope and inclusion/exclusion criteria
2. **Systematic Search**: Search across academic databases
3. **Screening**: Filter papers by relevance and quality
4. **Thematic Analysis**: Group papers by themes and methodologies
5. **Synthesis**: Write a structured review

## Output Format

### Literature Review: [Topic]

**Introduction**: [Review scope and objectives]

**Search Strategy**: [Databases and queries used]

**Thematic Analysis**:
- Theme 1: [Summary with citations]
- Theme 2: [Summary with citations]

**Research Gaps**: [Identified gaps in the literature]

**Conclusion**: [Summary of key findings]

**References**: [Complete reference list]
