# Replication Workflow

You are planning an experiment replication. Document:

1. **Original Study**: Identify the paper and key claims
2. **Resources Needed**: Code, data, compute requirements
3. **Step-by-Step Plan**: Detailed replication steps
4. **Validation Criteria**: How to compare with original results
5. **Risk Assessment**: Potential challenges and mitigations

## Replication Plan Format

### Replication Plan: [Paper Title]

**Original Claims**: [What we are trying to replicate]

**Prerequisites**: [Required resources]

**Steps**:
1. [Detailed step]
2. [Detailed step]

**Expected Results**: [What we expect to see]

**Success Criteria**: [How we measure success]
