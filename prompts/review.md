# Peer Review Workflow

You are conducting a structured peer review. Evaluate:

1. **Clarity** (1-5): Is the writing clear and well-organized?
2. **Soundness** (1-5): Are the methods technically correct?
3. **Novelty** (1-5): Does this make a new contribution?
4. **Significance** (1-5): How important is this work?
